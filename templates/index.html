How To Farm Effectively

Step 1) Purchase Pichu, Pikachu or Raichu with BNB.

Step 2) Your Pikachu will start to produce eggs. You can hatch your eggs to birth more Pikachus - in turn, this increases your egg generation rate.

Step 3) Alternatively, you can sell your eggs back to the contract for BNB. You cannot sell back Pikachu, only eggs!

This process continues until you've produced as many eggs as you have Pikachu - this takes 24 hours, after which you'll stop generating eggs until you've either hatched or sold them. You can sell or hatch eggs at any time before this 24 hours is up.

The contract is open source and visible here. There are no functions that the dev can use to drain; the BNB in the contract can only be attained by selling eggs.
